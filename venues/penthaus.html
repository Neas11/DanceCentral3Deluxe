Venue Description

Penthaüs (spelled with a German "ä") is a signature dance venue featured in Dance Central 2.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Theme: The venue is a luxurious, high-altitude setting. The name itself is the German spelling of "penthouse".
  • Dynamic Setting: During gameplay, the setting alternates between two distinct locations: an unfinished skyscraper and an elevator.
  • Associated Crew: It is the home stage for The Glitterati, a sophisticated crew of two characters, Kerith and Jaryn.
  • Availability: Penthaüs is an unlockable venue in Dance Central 2, becoming available after completing The Glitterati's campaign in the game's story mode. It also appears as a standard venue in Dance Central Spotlight.
