Venue Description

Lowdown is a signature dance venue introduced in Dance Central 2.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Theme: The venue is set in an underground urban environment.
  • Dynamic Setting: During gameplay, the setting alternates between two distinct locations: a subway station and the inside of a subway train.
  • Associated Crew: Lowdown is the primary venue for Hi-Def, a hip-hop-focused crew consisting of Mo and Glitch.
  • Cameos: Advertisements inside the subway train feature cameos from other characters, including Emilia and MacCoy.
