Venue Description

The Glitterati (Kerith and Jaryn) are closely associated with luxurious and elite settings, most notably the Penthaüs venue.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Themed Seating: As a high-end penthouse suite and skyscraper, the venue features modern, high-fashion decor.
  • Character Animation: In cutscenes and character introductions, The Glitterati are often depicted as aloof, high- fashion elitists. They may be seen posing in or near stylized furniture that reflects their wealthy, "runway-inspired" aesthetic.
  • Dynamic Setting: In Dance Central 2, the venue alternates between an unfinished skyscraper and an elevator, reinforcing the high-altitude, exclusive theme.
