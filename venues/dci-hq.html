Venue Description

DCI HQ (Dance Central Intelligence Headquarters) serves as the primary base of operations for the secret agency dedicated to stopping "dance crimes"

Visual and Functional Design

  • Aesthetic: The venue is sleek, sophisticated, and high-tech, reflecting the agency's modern 2010s era. Its primary color scheme is purple, matching the signature color of the DCI crew.
  • Time Machine: The facility houses a "convenient time machine" used by agents to travel to previous decades (70s, 80s, 90s, and 2000s) to learn historical dance crazes.
  • Control Center: It contains advanced computer systems that analyze criminal threats, such as Dr. Tan's mind-control helmets.
  • Training Floor: The venue doubles as a training ground where new recruits—including the player—are briefed and tested on their skills by lead task analysts Rasa and Lima.
