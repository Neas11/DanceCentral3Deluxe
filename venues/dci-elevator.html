Venue Description

An elevator is a key location featured in both Dance Central 2 and Dance Central 3, particularly associated with the elite "Glitterati" and the secret "DCI" agency.

Visual and Functional Design

  • The Story Context: At the beginning of Dance Central 3, the player is invited to a mysterious "underground party".
  • The Sequence: You start the game inside an elevator that descends toward the DCI HQ (Dance Central Intelligence headquarters).
  • The Meeting: When the doors open, you meet agents Rasa and Lima, who recruit you into the agency after you prove your skills on the spot.
  • Default Venue: DCI HQ serves as the primary venue for Rasa and Lima.
