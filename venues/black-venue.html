Venue Description

There is no official venue specifically named "The Black Venue" or similar.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Lowdown (Dance Central 2): This venue is set in an underground subway station and train car, featuring an urban, slightly gritty aesthetic that is more dimly lit than the others. It is the home base for the Hi-Def crew.
  • The Airship (Dance Central 2): Dr. Tan's mobile headquarters has an ominous, high-tech, and metallic interior that serves as the backdrop for the final boss battle of the game. It is designed to be imposing and dark, in contrast to the tropical or vibrant daytime venues.
  • Crow's Nest (Dance Central 3): This venue is a large outdoor arena with a gothic, industrial feel, serving as the stage for the antagonistic Murder of Crows crew. It has a darker atmosphere than most other locations.
