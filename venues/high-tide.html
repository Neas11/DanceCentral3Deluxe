Venue Description

High Tide is a primary venue featured in Dance Central 2.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Locations: The venue is unique because it alternates between two distinct areas: a beach and a nearby basketball court.
  • Associated Crew: It is the home venue for Riptide, the athletic and sporty crew represented by the characters Bodie and Emilia.
  • Availability: High Tide is available from the start of Dance Central 2.
  • Role in Gameplay: In the game's "Crew Challenge" (story mode), High Tide is typically the first venue players visit to begin their career and earn the right to represent the Riptide crew.
