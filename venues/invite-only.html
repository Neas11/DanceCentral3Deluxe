Venue Description

Invite Only is a residential-themed venue that serves as the home territory for the sister duo Flash4wrd (Taye and Li'l T)

Visual and Functional Design

  • Location: The venue is Taye and Li'l T's childhood home, identifiable by family photos of the sisters in the background.
  • Era: It represents the 1990s in the game's story mode.
  • Atmosphere: The setting is a casual house party with a "lived-in" feel. In the campaign, Taye and Li'l T are shown hosting frequent parties that reportedly annoy their parents and neighbors.
  • Role in Campaign: Agents from Dance Central Intelligence (DCI) travel back to this 90s setting to learn the era's signature moves from Flash4wrd.
