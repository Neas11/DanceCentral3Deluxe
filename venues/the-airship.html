Venue Description

The Airship is a major unlockable venue featured in Dance Central 2.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Theme: The venue is a massive, high-tech aircraft that serves as the mobile base for the series' primary antagonist, Dr. Tan.
  • Dynamic Setting: Like other venues in the second game, it alternates between two areas: the main control station and a platform on the exterior underside of the ship, which features large robotic arms.
  • Associated Crew: It is the home stage for D-Cypher, a crew of robots (CYPH-56 and CYPH-78) created by Dr. Tan to defeat the player.
