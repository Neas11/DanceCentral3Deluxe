Venue Description

Tee Off is a signature venue introduced in Dance Central 2.

Visual and Functional Design

  • Themed Setting: The venue is a miniature golf course that alternates between two distinct themed holes: a dinosaur- themed hole and an alien-themed hole.
  • Default Crew: It serves as the home venue for the Flash4wrd crew, which consists of the sister duo Taye and Li'l T.
  • Availability: Tee Off is available from the start of Dance Central 2.
