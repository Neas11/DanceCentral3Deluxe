Venue Description

The Airship is a high-tech venue introduced in Dance Central 2, there is no official venue specifically named "The Airship Dark."

Visual and Functional Design

  • Antagonist Base: It serves as the mobile fortress and headquarters for Dr. Tan.
  • Themed Areas: The venue features a high-tech interior with a command center and two large robotic arms that the player dances between.
  • Atmosphere: It has an ominous, technicolor, and metallic look, fitting for the final "boss" encounter of the game.
  • Destruction: In the Dance Central 2 storyline, once the player defeats the D-Cypher crew, the Airship begins to combust and is ultimately destroyed.
