Venue Description

Toprock Ave. is an urban street venue that serves as the home base for the breakdancing crew Hi-Def (Mo and Glitch)

Visual and Functional Design

  • Theme: The venue represents the 1980s era in the game's story mode and is designed as a city street corner under construction.
  • Visuals: The location features exposed brick walls, scaffolding, and graffiti on the back wall, including the "Hi Def" crew tag.
  • Name Origin: The name comes from "toprocking," a common style of breakdancing used in the game.
  • Details: The street signs at the corner read "Central Alley" and "Toprock Ave". A car resembling the 1964 Chevrolet Suburban from the movie Back to the Future is also present, a nod to the 80s time-travel theme.
