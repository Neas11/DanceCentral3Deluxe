Venue Description

Crow's Nest is an unlockable venue in the video game Dance Central 3, and not a real-world location associated with a dance central business or event. It is the default venue for the in-game crew "Murder of Crows"

Trivia

In the song data of Dance Central 3, the Crow's Nest is named "throneroom".
