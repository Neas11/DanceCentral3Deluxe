Venue Description

Free Skate is a vibrant roller-skating rink venue that serves as the home territory for Lu$h Crew (Angel and Miss Aubrey).

Visual and Functional Design

  • Theme: The venue is a retro roller arena heavily inspired by the 1970s disco era.
  • Setting: It features a polished wooden skating floor surrounded by a tiered spectator area.
  • Aesthetic: The rink is characterized by era-appropriate decor, including colorful lighting, disco-themed patterns, and a lively, nostalgic atmosphere.
