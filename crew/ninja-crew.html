Ninja Crew

The Ninja Crew is one of the returning dance crews in Dance Central 3. They are a stylish, mysterious, and disciplined duo known for their sharp movements, martial‑arts‑inspired poses, and sleek aesthetic. Both characters appear in Dance Central 2 and Dance Central 3.

Ninja Crew Members

Kichi

  • First appearance: Dance Central 2
  • Playable in: DC2 and DC3
  • Crew: Ninja Crew
  • Partner: Shinju
    • Birthday: July 24
  • Known for:
  • Calm, focused demeanor
  • Precise, sharp dance style
  • Traditional ninja‑inspired outfits

Shinju

  • First appearance: Dance Central 2
  • Playable in: DC2 and DC3
  • Crew: Ninja Crew
  • Partner: Kichi
  • Known for:
    • Graceful, fluid movement
    • A more elegant, flowing ninja aesthetic
    • Balanced contrast to Kichi’s sharper style
