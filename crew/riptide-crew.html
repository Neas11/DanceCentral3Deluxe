Riptide Crew

Riptide is one of the major dance crews featured in Dance Central 3. They are known for their sporty, athletic energy, upbeat personalities, and powerful choreography. The crew consists of Emilia and Bodie, two dancers who bring a competitive, outdoorsy vibe to the Dance Central universe.

Riptide Crew Members

Emilia

  • One of the two main dancers of Riptide.
  • Known for:
  • Athletic, energetic movement
  • Confident, upbeat personality
  • Strong, grounded hip‑hop style
  • Appears in multiple gameplay videos, including spectator mode performances.

Bodie

  • The second half of Riptide.
  • Known for:
    • Powerful, muscular dance style
    • Sporty, competitive attitude
    • Strong stage presence
    • Featured in mixed‑reality cosplay videos dancing to tracks like “I Am the Best”.
