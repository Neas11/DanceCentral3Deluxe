Murder of Crows

The Murder of Crows (often abbreviated M.O.C.) is the villain crew of Dance Central 3. They are the only crew aligned with Tan Industries, and they play a major role in the game’s story, especially in the final chapters.

Murder of Crows Crew Members

Dr. Tan

  • The overarching villain of the Dance Central series.
  • Known for:
  • Founder of Tan Industries.
  • Uses mind control technology to manipulate dancers.
  • In DC3, he forces Oblio to cooperate with him for his own gain.

Oblio

  • He is being mind‑controlled by Dr. Tan His cooperation with the Murder of Crows is not voluntary
  • Known for:
    • Dr. Tan’s son.
    • A core character in the series.
    • Initially appears aloof and mysterious.
    • Eventually breaks free from Dr. Tan’s control.
