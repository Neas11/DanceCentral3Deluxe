D-Cypher Elite

D‑Cypher Elite is the final boss crew of Dance Central 2. They are an upgraded, more dangerous version of the original robotic crew D‑Cypher, created by the series villain Dr. Tan. They appear only in Dance Central 2, not in DC3.

D-Cypher Elite Crew Members

CYPH‑ELITE

  • The newest and most advanced robot created by Dr. Tan.
  • Designed to surpass CYPH‑56 and CYPH‑78.
  • Represents the “elite” version of the D‑Cypher line.
  • Appears in the final Crew Challenge.

    • Dr. Tan

  • The main antagonist of the Dance Central series.
  • Represents the crew alongside CYPH‑ELITE.
  • Controls the robots and challenges the player in the final showdown.
