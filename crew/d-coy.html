D-Coy

D‑Coy is a returning dance crew in Dance Central 3, featuring two fan‑favorite characters from the original Dance Central: Dare and MacCoy. They are known for their quirky personalities, street‑style hip‑hop, and playful, expressive choreography.They are also an unlockable crew in DC3.Appears in both Dance Central and Dance Central 3. Together, Dare and MacCoy form D‑Coy, a crew built around fun, personality, and expressive hip‑hop.
