The Glitterati

The Glitterati are one of the most recognizable and stylish dance crews in the Dance Central series. They are a glamorous, high‑fashion duo known for their dramatic flair, elite attitude, and over‑the‑top couture. In Dance Central 3, they return with new outfits, new story scenes, and new routines.

The Glitterati Crew Members

Kerith

  • One half of The Glitterati.
  • Known for:
  • Fierce, confident attitude
  • High‑fashion poses and sharp lines
  • Dramatic, couture‑inspired outfits
  • Wears elaborate, metallic, sparkly costumes in DC3, including the “Haute Blooded” variant.

Jaryn

  • The other half of The Glitterati.
  • Known for:
    • Smooth, stylish movement
    • A cool, aloof personality
    • Fashion‑forward looks with bold silhouettes
    • Notable for wearing a “waistcape” (nicknamed the “asscape”) in the Haute Blooded outfit.
