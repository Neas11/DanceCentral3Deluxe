Flash4wrd

Flash4wrd is the 1990s dance crew in Dance Central 3, featuring Taye and Li'l T, two stylish, high‑energy dancers who embody the bold, neon‑soaked attitude of the decade. They are known for their fierce personalities, girl‑power energy, and flashy choreography. Together, they form a duo that Harmonix describes as: “Cool and sassy… the Flash4wrd girls throw the meanest house parties around.”

Flash4wrd Crew Members

Taye

  • Originally appeared in the earliest Dance Central lore as a solo dancer before forming Flash4wrd.
  • Known for:
  • Confident, sassy attitude
  • Strong hip‑hop foundationr
  • Bright, expressive fashion
  • Being the “leader” personality of the duo

Li'l T

  • Introduced alongside Taye as part of Flash4wrd.
  • Known for:
    • Edgy, bold style
    • High‑energy moves
    • A more aggressive, sharp dance approach
