Icon Crew

The Icon Crew is one of the unlockable dance crews in Dance Central 3. They are a stylish, modern, high‑energy duo known for their sleek choreography, futuristic fashion, and confident personalities. They represent the “modern pop star” aesthetic of the Dance Central universe. They originally debuted in Dance Central 2 and return in DC3 as unlockable dancers. Together, they form Icon, a crew built around star power, precision, and modern pop performance.
