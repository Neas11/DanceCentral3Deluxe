Hi-Def

Hi‑Def is one of the returning dance crews in Dance Central 3, featuring Mo and Glitch, two fan‑favorite characters known for their upbeat personalities and high‑energy hip‑hop style. They originally appeared in Dance Central 2 and return in DC3 as part of the time‑traveling storyline. Together, they form Hi‑Def, a crew that blends smooth robotics with energetic hip‑hop.
