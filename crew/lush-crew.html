Lush Crew

Lush Crew is one of the returning dance crews in Dance Central 3, featuring two iconic characters from the earliest days of the series: Angel and Miss Aubrey. They represent elegance, glamour, and high‑energy club style, blending ballet‑trained precision with Latin flair.Together, they form Lush Crew, a stylish, high‑energy duo with a polished aesthetic.

Lush Crew Members

Angel

    • First appeared in the original Dance Central
    • Known for:
      • Latin‑inspired movement
      • Smooth, controlled style
      • Athletic physique (notably referenced by Harmonix as “enviable ab muscles”)
    • Default dancer for the fan‑favorite track “Poison” at conventions.

      • Miss Aubrey

    • Also debuted in the original Dance Central.
    • Known for:
      • Ballet training

      • Iconic fashion sense (her red suspenders became a cosplay staple)
