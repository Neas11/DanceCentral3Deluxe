Character Bio

Rasa is one of five characters to only appear in one Dance Central game, the others being Eliot in Dance Central, CYPH-ELITE in Dance Central 2, Lima in Dance Central 3, and Hart in Dance Central (VR).

  • Dance Style: 2010s / Pop
  • Personality: Bold, confident, energetic
  • Vibe: High-energy performance king
